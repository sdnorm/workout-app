# Equipment Profiles

## Home Gym

### Available Equipment
- Adjustable dumbbells (5-52.5 lbs)
- Kettlebell (35 lbs)
- Pull-up bar (doorframe mounted)
- Resistance bands (light, medium, heavy)
- Ab wheel
- Stability ball
- Flat/incline adjustable bench

### Exercise Constraints
- No barbell or heavy barbells
- No cable machines
- No leg press, leg extension, or leg curl machines
- No pec deck or other plate-loaded machines
- Limited max weight (2x 52.5 lb dumbbells = 105 lbs total)

### Best Suited For
- Upper body dumbbell work
- Bodyweight movements (push-ups, pull-ups, dips)
- Single-leg work (Bulgarian split squats, lunges, step-ups)
- Core work
- Light to moderate lower body

## Gym

### Available Equipment
- Full dumbbell rack (5-100+ lbs)
- Barbells (Olympic, EZ curl)
- Cable machines (adjustable pulleys)
- Leg press
- Leg extension / Leg curl machines
- Pec deck / Reverse pec deck
- Lat pulldown / Seated row
- Squat rack with safety bars
- Smith machine
- Various benches (flat, incline, decline)
- Pull-up bars
- Hack squat machine
- Calf raise machines (standing, seated, donkey)

### Best Suited For
- Heavy compound lifts
- Full exercise variety
- Machine-based isolation work
- Progressive overload with smaller weight increments
- All muscle groups at all volume levels
